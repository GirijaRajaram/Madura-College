Examination Section

                        The Examination section is headed by a Controller of Examinations. He is assisted by an Assistant Controller of Examinations and supporting staff.
The Examiners for setting the question papers are selected by the Controller from the panel, submitted by the departments, of teachers from other Colleges and Universities.

The College follows double valuation of answer scripts both at UG and PG levels. The Controller appoints internal and external examiners from the panel of examiners provided by the Boards of studies and approved by the Academic Council. The answer scripts are independently valued by the internal and external examiners and the average marks are awarded to the students. In case of more than 10% difference in marks between the two valuations, the papers are referred to third valuation. The internal examiner is generally the course teacher. The results are published with the approval of the Awards Committee, consisting of two University nominees and selected senior faculty members. The College offers the facility of re valuation of answer scripts if needed by the students.

After the publication of the results of even semester examinations, a supplementary examination is conducted for the benefit of students who have failed in the final semester. Students with only one paper arrear in earlier semesters are also offered this facility. This enables the students to get the degree without much loss of time.
