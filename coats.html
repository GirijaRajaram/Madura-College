Coat of Arms

           The Sanskrit motto “VIDHYA DHARMENA SHOBATHE” means “Learning Shines with Righteousness”. The Lion stands for power and it is the vehicle on which the Goddess Sakthi rode when she slew Mahishasura, the embodiment of Tamas. The elephant head stands for Sri Ganesha, the God of Wisdom and Success. The lotuses on either side, one white and the other red, symbolize the rise of the human spirit from the muddy bottom of our lower nature to achieve Purity and Renunciation, respectively. The Pranava is the coping stone of the entire arch of human progress.

           The College Colours are White, Blue and Yellow, symbolizing Purity, Love and Sacrifice, respectively.
