Milestones

       Some of the noteworthy highlights of the College

  1. College started in the year 1889

  2. One of the first colleges to be conferred the autonomous status in 1978-1979

  3. Re -Accreditated  by NAAC with ‘A’ Grade (CGPA of 3.32) in 2010

  4. Adoption of the Choice Based Credit System for UG courses in 2005

  5. Adoption of the revised Choice Based Credit System for UG & PG courses in 2008

  6. Funded projects from UGC, DST, and CSIR

  7. Campus networking, internet facilities in  the entire campus, computerized library transactions, and office automation

  8. A well equipped computing centre with internet facility

  9. Harvey Library housing about 75000 volumes

  10. Departmental libraries

  11. Recognised Research Centres leading to Ph.D in :                                      
    Zoology, Tamil ,  Botany,   Philosophy,   Physics

       The Mile Stones

1919       - The present premises was purchased.

1925      - College became a First grade College.

1937       - College moved to the present premises.

1941       - P.M.Sankara Iyer Hall came into existence.

1946-47 - College got affiliation for B.A. Mathematics and B.A. Economics.

1947-48 - College got affiliation for B.Com.

1949-50- College got affiliation for B.Sc Maths & Physics.

1950-51 - College got affiliation for B.Sc. Chemistry.

1947        - The T.V.S. Hostel came up thanks to the benevolence of T.V.Sundaram Iyengar & Sons.

1949        - Harvey Library was built thanks to the benevolence of Madura Mills.

1950        - The Gajana Babu Rao Hostel was constructed with the contribution from his family.

1956        - The Pre University Course was started as per the revised regulations of The Madras University.

1957        - The Three Year Degree Course was introduced.

  1. B.A. Economics
  2. B.Com.
  3. B.Sc. Mathematics
  4. B.Sc. Physics
  5. B.Sc. Chemistry &
  6. B.Sc. Botany

1958       - M.Sc. Mathematics and M.Sc. Physics were started.

1959       - M.Sc. Chemistry was introduced.

1989-90 - College celebrated its Centenary.

1997-98 - College became a Co-educational institution.

2003        - NAAC Accredidation at B++.

2003        - The assistance of Constituency Development Fund made available to the college by Sri.P.Mohan, Member of Parliament, the ground floor of the Mohan block was                       constructed.

2004        - The first floor of The Mohan Block was constructed with assistance from the Parliamentary Funds made available to the college by Sri.Cho S.Ramasami, Member                       of Rajya Sabha.

2005        - Integrated Ramanujan Computer Training and Browsing Centre was built by the Management.

2005        - A building with around 2100 sq.ft. area is built near the Duraisamy Block, thanks to Mr.M.Krishnamoorthy, a Philanthropist of Madurai.

2006        - The Health Centre and The Sky Watcher’s Room were constructed with the assistance of UGC X Plan Fund.

2009        - In February Autonomous status was extended upto 2014

                  The College was re-accredited by NAAC with ‘A’ grade with a CGPA of 3.32

2010-11 - Starting of M.Phil programmes in Mathematics & Economics from the academic year.
