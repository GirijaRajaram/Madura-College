Department of Computer Science

ESTD - 1987

Mission & Vision


  • To design and update the curriculum, bearing in mind the relevance to regional and national requirements and expectations.

  • To impart quality education in the field of computer science, enabling our students to confidently face the job market.

  • To inculcate the spirit of scientific temper in the minds of the students.

    •


Courses offered

The Department of Computer Science was established in the year 1987 to impart the knowledge of Computer Science & Information Technology. It offers UG and PG Courses besides PG Diploma in Computer Applications.

Course highlights

The course curriculum is designed in a well structured manner by the Experts in the field of CS & IT to match the growing needs and includes the latest trends to meet the changing world. During the final semester project work is compulsory. The students are encouraged to take up projects with leading organizations in IT sector.

Laboratory

The department of computer science has a full fledged laboratory of having many IBM P IV machines with high configurations to help the students learn more technical details. The department library has a very good collection of more than 4000 standard books on all topic related to computer field. The Library functions on "open access" system.

Club Activities

The "ADA CLUB" is an association formed for the students, by the Students and of the Students to encourage the students in Socio-educative aspects. The Club arranges Seminars and Guest Lectures by Eminent Professors/technocrats in the field of CS & IT to know the recent and emerging trends. Research activities

The staff of the Department guide M.Phil project work of students of MKU, BDU and MSU.

Other activities

The Department runs the Bharathidasan University study centre for MCA, MS(IT) and M.Phil (CS).
