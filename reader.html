READER’S FORUM



                        Reader’s Forum was incepted in the The Madura College by the Department of Philosophy on 11th Sep 2006 with Dr R.Murali, the Head of the Philosophy Department as the staff-advisory and student coordinators Ms.M.Susithra*, research scholar and Ms.S.Suganthi*, M.A. English student. It is designed as a space to sharpen minds. Students and staff from all departments of the college and academicians and students from other educational institutions take part in the forum to taste the joy of reading. A Book or any work on social, cultural, literary, political, historical, philosophical, psychological, science & technological spheres are reviewed and discussed as the initial part of the programme. Articles to enhance and enrich knowledge are chosen and read in the second part. Until the end of year 2006 the forum was conducted on every Mondays and Fridays. Research scholars, students and teachers and even outsiders were among the reviewers.

                            Fifty-four meets have been conducted so far in our Readers’ Forum. Some of the world’s best works in various subjects have been placed open for discussion. Especially the forum feels proud to state that our enthusiastic readers have reviewed the writings of Noble Laureates, Booker-Prize winners and Sahitya Academy winners. Individuals from different walks of life have participated in the program and have rendered their unique presentations and made their valid contributions during the discussion. Besides our College students, even other college students have also reviewed various classical works is a point worth to be noted here. The Readers Forum is thus a groundbreaking event in the history of the Madura College. It stirs and will continue to scintillate and sensitize sensible beings throughout its journey.
