Youth Red Cross



                        This unit has been rendering its service to the needy and to the students. The unit has a total membership of 100 students, and one office bearer.

The Objectives of YRC:


  • Promotion and protection of health and life.
  • Selfless services to the sick and suffering.
  • Promotion of national and international friendship.
  • Disaster relief to the victims.

    • Activities of YRC:


  • Volunteers have donated blood for the needy.
  • Awareness program on blood donation is regularly conducted to motivate the students.
  • AIDS awareness workshop
  • Planting of saplings in and around the campus
  • Anti-Smoking and Anti-Drug Campaign
  • First-aid Training Program
  • Production of documentaries on health hazards
  • elping the visually challenged students to record their lessons on cassette
  • A proposal to build a ramp for the disabled students in the college main building with the consensus of the management

    • Hospital Service:

                            There are plans to continue this service on weekends at Government Hospitals.

    Community Service:

                            Constant support has been given to under-privileged children by providing them with notebooks and other learning aids.
    A number of health related meetings and distribution of pamphlets related to health are conducted.
