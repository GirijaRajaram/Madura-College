The College is administered by the Madura College Board, as also are the Sethupathi Higher Secondary School, Madura College Higher Secondary School and the Town Primary School. The Madura College Board elects a Board of Directors consisting of twelve members. From among the twelve Directors one President, one Vice President, one Secretary, one Joint Secretary and one Treasurer are elected.
