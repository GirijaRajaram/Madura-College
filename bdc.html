Acitivities of blood donors club (BDC):


                        The Blood Donor Club of our College is doing an exemplary service to the Society in blood donation. Blood is donated mainly to the unknown suffers.

The activities of the Blood Donors Club are two-fold:


  • To conduct blood donation camps inside the college campus to motivate and induce new Blood donors and
  • To respond to emergency calls from hospitals by identifying and deputing students of particular Blood Group.

    •                         The Blood Donors Club is functioning for the past 3 Decades. By donating maximum units of Blood each year, the College has won and retained the Rolling Shield for the years right from 1980 to 1993. It is a rare honour that our Club has received the shield for 11th time for donating more number of blood units to the Blood bank of GR Hospital from 1997 to 2009. Apart from this, the College has won to its credit several others rolling shields.

                            Every academic year, the BDC conducts on an average 6 camps, contributing about 350 units of blood to the blood bank of Govt. Rajaji Hospital, Madurai.

                            The BDC justifies the above motto of blood donation movement and helps to save hundreds of lives in Madurai city.
